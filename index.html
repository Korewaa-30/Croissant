🥐 Croissant

"Croissant" is a buttery, flaky, and crescent-shaped pastry. It's a popular French pastry known for its delicious taste and distinctive layered texture. Croissants are often enjoyed as a breakfast item or snack and can be filled with various ingredients like chocolate or almond paste. They are made from layered yeast-leavened dough and butter, which is folded and rolled several times to create the characteristic flaky layers.
